While you are in town, be sure to check out the rest of what Detroit has to offer. From rich history and vibrant culture, to incredible shopping and dining experiences, the Motor City has you covered.

## Art & Culture

Detroit is home to world-class art institutions and cultural landmarks that reflect the city's creative spirit.

:::grid{columns=2}
:::card{title="Detroit Institute of Arts"}
<div style="text-align: center; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <img src="/images/travel/dia.jpg" alt="Detroit Institute of Arts" style="width: 100%; max-width: 600px; height: auto;" />
</div>

One of the premier art museums in the United States, featuring Diego Rivera's iconic Detroit Industry Murals and an extensive collection spanning centuries and continents.

[Visit Website](https://dia.org)
:::

:::card{title="Henry Ford Museum"}
<div style="text-align: center; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <img src="/images/travel/henryford.jpg" alt="Henry Ford Museum" style="width: 100%; max-width: 600px; height: auto;" />

Explore American innovation and ingenuity at The Henry Ford Museum in nearby Dearborn. Home to the Rosa Parks bus, the Kennedy limousine, and countless artifacts celebrating American history and innovation.

[Visit Website](https://www.thehenryford.org)
:::
:::

[**Charles H. Wright Museum of African American History**](https://thewright.org): The world's largest museum dedicated to African American culture and history.

## Detroit Riverwalk

<div style="text-align: center; margin: 2rem auto; max-width: 1000px;">
  <img src="/images/travel/riverwalk.jpg" alt="Detroit Riverwalk" style="width: 100%; height: auto; border-radius: 8px;" />
  <p style="font-style: italic; margin-top: 0.5rem; color: #666;">The award-winning Detroit RiverWalk with views of the international waterfront</p>
</div>

Stretching 3.5 miles along the Detroit River, the award-winning [Detroit RiverWalk](https://detroitriverfront.org) offers stunning views of the international waterfront and Canada across the river.  Perfect for a morning jog, evening stroll, or bike ride, the riverwalk features:

- Beautiful waterfront parks and green spaces
- Public art installations
- Cafes and seasonal vendors
- The Dequindre Cut, a converted railroad line turned urban greenway
- International views of Windsor, Ontario

:::highlight
The River Walk connects the conference venue Huntington Place with conference hotel, Detroit Marriott at the Renaissance Center,making it a scenic route for your commute to and from the conference.**
:::

## Dining & Shopping

From innovative farm-to-table restaurants to classic Detroit-style pizza, the city's culinary scene is thriving. Explore [Eastern Market](https://www.easternmarket.org) for fresh local produce and artisan goods, or discover unique boutiques in the Midtown and Downtown districts.
