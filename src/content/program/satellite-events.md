IPAC’27 is pleased to offer meeting rooms for satellite events held in conjunction with the conference.

## Room Availability

- Meeting rooms are available free of charge for non-commercial events, subject to availability.
- For satellite events of up to 50 participants, IPAC’27 can provide a basic room setup with Table and Chair at no cost.


### Additional Services or Equipments

Additional equipment or services may be arranged upon request and will incur extra charges. These may include:

* TV or projector
* Coffee and snacks
* Catering or other food service

## Support
Please contact the [IPAC'27 Satellite Event Coordinator](/contact) if you have questions.
